Idukki

Idukki the landlocked hill district in Western Ghat is a place blessed with beauty and tranquility. Idukki one of Kerala’s nature rich areas with the land of massive mountains, green valleys and splendid streams located in the southern portion of Kerala is a paradise of nature and animal lovers.

Munnar

Munnar, South India’s most sought after hill station is not only a favorite tourist spot but also the centre of tea estates in Kerala. Munnar, an exotic hill station with enclosed calm of serenity and beauty is the most visited destination in South India.

Iravikulam National Park

This national park protects wild animals at its best. This national park is situated at an elevation of 7000 feet above the sea level. This sanctuary is famous Nilgiri tahr, a highly endangered wild goat. There are many kinds of rare flora in this hills.

Devikulam

The most sought after destination in Munnar, Devikulam is an ideal hill station with exotic flora and fauna and the cool mountain breeze. The Sita Devi Lake in hear is an ideal picnic spot with its charming ambiance and mineral rich water.
